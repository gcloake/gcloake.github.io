<div class="message">
Today I saw the seasons  </br>
for the very first time. </br></br>

A year inside  </br>
to open my eyes  </br>
now watch  </br>
the clematis climb. </br></br>

Stop,  </br>
and *see* the seasons!  </br>
Breathe,  </br>
take heed  </br>
of time.
</div>
